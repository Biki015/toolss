Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our Word to PDF converter

Is this service really free to use?

Yes, our Word to PDF converter is completely free to use with no hidden charges. You can convert as many documents as you need without any limitations.

What Word file formats are supported?

We support both .doc (Microsoft Word 97-2003) and .docx (Microsoft Word 2007 and later) file formats. The maximum file size is 25MB.

How does the image compression work?

Our intelligent compression analyzes each image in your document and applies optimized compression based on your selected quality level. This reduces file size while maintaining visual quality.

Is my document secure during conversion?

Absolutely. Your documents are processed securely using SSL encryption and are automatically deleted from our servers immediately after conversion. We never store your files permanently.

Can I convert password-protected Word files?

Currently, we don't support password-protected Word documents. Please remove the password protection before uploading your file for conversion.
